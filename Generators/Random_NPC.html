Random NPC Generator

This generator will generate n NPCs based on what is input into NPC Quantity.


Real-world Data uses slightly modified US population distribution by age data obtained from infoplease.com. The US Life Expectancy at Birth (LEB) is set to 80 years. The table can be seen below.
Uniform Probabilites rolls a die to determine the age with as many sides equal to the race's Life Expectancy at Birth (LEB). This ensures each age has the same odds of being selected.

Source #1: infoplease.com - United States Demographics Statistics
Source #2: Official 5e Books - Race entries to gather maximum age and adulthood age
