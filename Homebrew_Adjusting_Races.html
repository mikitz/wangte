Adjusting Races

  • All Ability scores are interchangable
    • E.G. you can adjust the Ability scores of your selected race to be whatever you want as long as you only have one at +2 and one other at +1.
  • All cultural bonuses are interchangeable as long as the above conditions are satisfied
  • Physiological traits cannot be moved

EXAMPLE

You can move an entire race’s stat block, excluding physiological traits (like water breathing, fire breath, etc.), to any other race in any source book. 

You want to have the weapon proficiencies from the elf and to put your +2 into Strength and +1 in Dexterity. You can do this as ANY race in any source book. Want it as a dwarf? Okay. As a tiefling? Okay. You pick.

Want to take all Variant Human features and put them on an elf? Okay! Or put them on a tiefling? Do it! What about a mountain dwarf? YES!
