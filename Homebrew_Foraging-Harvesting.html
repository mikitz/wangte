Foraging/Harvesting

Survival Check to find them

  • nat 1: You find no herbs
  • 5+: 1d4+nature
  • 10+: 1d6+nature
  • 15+: 1d8+nature
  • 20+: 1d10+nature
  • 25+: 1d12+nature
  • 30+: 1d20+nature
  • nat 20: Max whatever die you get based on modifiers, then roll again

Modifiers based on creature size: (x10 for small things, like dragon scales)

  • Tiny /4
  • Small /2
  • Medium x1
  • Large x2
  • Huge x4 
  • Gargantuan x8
