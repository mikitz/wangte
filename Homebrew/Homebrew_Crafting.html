Crafting

Gold per Day

  • The amount of gold one can produce in a day is equal to your proficiency bonus (assuming you have proficiency in the relevant tool) plus your Intelligence modifier, then times that sum by 10.
    • E.G. I have a proficiency bonus of +7 and an Int. mod. of +5. So, I add 7 and 5 to get 12, then multiply it by 10, equalling 120gp of crafting per 8 hours of work per day.

Crafting Constructs

  • Requirements
    • Control Gem with a CR limit equal to or greater than the Creature
    • Materials equal to Creature CR x 100 gp
  • Repairing
    • Depends on size
      • TIny: 15 seconds per HP
      • Small: 30 seconds per HP
      • Medium: 1 minute per HP
      • Large: 2 minutes per HP
      • Huge: 4 minutes per HP
      • Gargantuan: 8 minutes per HP
  • Can craft creatures with a CR equal to the Artificer’s level
