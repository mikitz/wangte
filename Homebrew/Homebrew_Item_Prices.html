Turning a Spell into a Magic Item

Instructions

  • 1 spell scroll per charge
  • 1 item of your choosing
  • A spellcaster with access to said spell
    • 1 spell slot equal to the number of charges must be used to enchant the item per charge
      1. if the number of charges is more than 9, then spell slots can be combined with their sum equalling the total number of charge

Crafting Duration

  • 8 hours per spell slot level per charge

Calculating Cost

  • Spell scrolls (See Spell Scrolls table above.)
  • Spell slots (See Cost (For Service))
