Ship Navigation

  • Navigation Check (1d20 + Wis. Mod.  + Prof.)
  • Rules:
    • One check is made per day. Should the check fall below the DC,
      the sailing for the day is done in a random direction every 8 hours determined by 1d6 for each set of 8 hours,
      where 1 represents heading upper right on       The Sea of Fallen Stars scene hex map,
      then counting up and continuing clockwise for the remaining directions and dice faces.
    • Should one get lost and find themselves able to see land, a DC 15 Widsom (Navigation) Check  9 is rolled to find your place on your charts.
      Once you discover your position on the charts, you plot a new course successfullly by passing a DC 10 Wisdom (Navigation) Check        4.
  • Random Directions:
    • Hours 1-8: Dir -  1 S:24 N:24 F:48 miles
    • Hours 9-16: Dir -  3 S:24 N:24 F:48 miles
    • Hours 17-24: Dir -  2 S:24 N:24 F:48 miles
  • Difficulty Classes:
    • Fast Pace: +2
    • Slow Pace: -2
    • DC 20 - Stormy or foggy and no land in sight.
    • DC 15 - Stormy or foggy and land in sight.
    • DC 15 - Overcast skies and no land in sight.
    • DC 12 - Scattered Clouds and no lind in sight.
    • DC 10 - Clear skies and no land in sight.
    • DC N/A - Overcast skies and land in sight.
    • DC N/A - Clear skies and land in sight.
