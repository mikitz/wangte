Negotiating a Price

  • You make one Persuasion (Charisma) check per store, which means your check applies to all items you, or your obvious group, purchase at the shop.
  • Difficulty Class (DC) Outcomes
    • DC - 10+, the price goes up by 25% (Multiply the price by 1.25)
    • DC - 5-9, the price goes up by 10% (Multiply the price by 1.1)
    • DC +/- 4, price stays the same
    • DC + 5-9, price goes down by 10% (Multiply the price by 0.9)
    • DC + 10+, price goes down by 25% (Multiply the price by 0.75)
