Gold per Day

  • The amount of gold one can produce in a day is equal to your proficiency bonus (assuming you have proficiency in the relevant tool) plus your Intelligence modifier, then times that sum by 10.
    • E.G. I have a proficiency bonus of +7 and an Int. mod. of +5. So, I add 7 and 5 to get 12, then multiply it by 10, equalling 120gp of crafting per 8 hours of work per day.
