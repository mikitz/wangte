Some notes about the Calendar of Harptos: There are 3 weeks in a month and each week has 10 days with 12 months in the year.

Employees


Input the quantity of differnt types of employees that you have. E.G. if you have 7 brewmasters and 3 barmaids, enter 2.

Miscellaneous Expenses


Input the quantity of differnt types of miscellaneous expenses that your business has. E.G. if your business must pay taxes and purchase ingredients, enter 2.
